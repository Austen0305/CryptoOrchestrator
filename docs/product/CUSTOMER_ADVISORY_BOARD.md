# Customer Advisory Board (CAB) Program

**Last Updated**: December 12, 2025

## Overview

The Customer Advisory Board (CAB) is a strategic program that brings together key customers to provide feedback, guidance, and insights on product direction, features, and priorities.

---

## Objectives

1. **Gather Strategic Feedback**: Collect insights on product direction and priorities
2. **Validate Roadmap**: Validate product roadmap with key customers
3. **Build Relationships**: Strengthen relationships with key customers
4. **Drive Innovation**: Identify new opportunities and use cases
5. **Improve Product-Market Fit**: Ensure product meets customer needs

---

## Board Structure

### Board Composition

**Size**: 8-12 members (optimal for engagement)

**Member Criteria**:
- Active users with significant usage
- Diverse use cases and industries
- Mix of enterprise and individual users
- Geographic diversity
- Willingness to provide feedback

**Term Length**: 12 months (renewable)

**Meeting Frequency**: Quarterly (4 meetings per year)

---

## Member Selection

### Selection Process

1. **Nomination**: Internal team nominates candidates
2. **Invitation**: Send formal invitation to selected candidates
3. **Onboarding**: Welcome new members, provide context
4. **Orientation**: Introduce program structure and expectations

### Member Benefits

- **Early Access**: Preview of new features
- **Direct Influence**: Shape product roadmap
- **Networking**: Connect with other key customers
- **Recognition**: Featured on website (optional)
- **Exclusive Content**: Access to product insights

---

## Meeting Structure

### Quarterly Meetings

**Format**: Virtual or in-person (hybrid option)

**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Agenda Template**:
1. **Welcome & Introductions** (10 min)
2. **Product Updates** (20 min)
   - Recent releases
   - Upcoming features
   - Roadmap preview
3. **Strategic Discussion** (60 min)
   - Product direction
   - Feature priorities
   - Market trends
4. **Feedback Session** (40 min)
   - Feature feedback
   - Pain points
   - Improvement suggestions
5. **Q&A & Wrap-up** (20 min)

### Between-Meeting Engagement

- **Surveys**: Quarterly feedback surveys
- **Beta Testing**: Early access to beta features
- **Direct Communication**: Dedicated channel for feedback
- **Office Hours**: Optional monthly office hours

---

## Topics & Discussions

### Strategic Topics

- Product roadmap and priorities
- Market trends and opportunities
- Competitive landscape
- Industry challenges
- Use case evolution

### Tactical Topics

- Feature requests and feedback
- User experience improvements
- Integration needs
- Performance requirements
- Security and compliance

---

## Feedback Collection

### Methods

1. **Meeting Discussions**: Structured discussions during meetings
2. **Surveys**: Quarterly feedback surveys
3. **Beta Testing**: Feedback on beta features
4. **Direct Communication**: Ongoing feedback channel
5. **User Interviews**: One-on-one interviews (optional)

### Feedback Processing

1. **Collection**: Gather all feedback
2. **Categorization**: Organize by theme/priority
3. **Analysis**: Identify patterns and insights
4. **Action Planning**: Create action items
5. **Follow-up**: Communicate outcomes to board

---

## Success Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

- **Meeting Attendance**: Target 80%+
- **Survey Response Rate**: Target 70%+
- **Beta Participation**: Target 60%+

### Impact Metrics

- **Feature Adoption**: Features influenced by CAB
- **Roadmap Alignment**: % of roadmap items validated by CAB
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS of CAB members
- **Retention**: Retention rate of CAB members

---

## Program Management

### Responsibilities

**Product Team**:
- Plan and facilitate meetings
- Collect and analyze feedback
- Communicate outcomes
- Manage program logistics

**Customer Success**:
- Member selection and onboarding
- Relationship management
- Ongoing engagement

**Executive Team**:
- Strategic participation
- High-level updates
- Relationship building

---

## Best Practices

### Meeting Facilitation

- **Prepare**: Send agenda and materials in advance
- **Engage**: Encourage participation from all members
- **Document**: Take detailed notes
- **Follow-up**: Share outcomes and action items

### Member Engagement

- **Value**: Ensure members see value
- **Respect**: Respect members' time
- **Act**: Act on feedback when possible
- **Communicate**: Keep members informed

### Feedback Integration

- **Prioritize**: Prioritize feedback based on impact
- **Validate**: Validate with broader user base
- **Implement**: Implement high-impact feedback
- **Credit**: Acknowledge CAB contributions
