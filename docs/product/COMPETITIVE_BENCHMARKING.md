# Competitive Benchmarking Guide

**Last Updated**: December 12, 2025

## Overview

Competitive benchmarking helps us understand our position in the market, identify opportunities, and make informed product decisions.

---

## Objectives

1. **Market Understanding**: Understand competitive landscape
2. **Feature Gaps**: Identify feature gaps and opportunities
3. **Positioning**: Inform product positioning
4. **Best Practices**: Learn from competitors
5. **Strategic Planning**: Guide strategic planning

---
