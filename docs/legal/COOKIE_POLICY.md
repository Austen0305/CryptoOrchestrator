# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated**: December 12, 2025

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide and improve our services.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:
- **Authentication**: Keeping you logged in
- **Preferences**: Remembering your settings
- **Analytics**: Understanding how you use our platform
- **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud
- **Functionality**: Enabling features to work properly

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies
- Required for the platform to function
- Cannot be disabled
- Examples: Authentication tokens, session IDs

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
- Remember your preferences
- Enable enhanced features
- Can be disabled (may affect functionality)

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies
- Help us understand usage patterns
- Improve user experience
- Can be disabled

### 3.4 Marketing Cookies
- Used for advertising (if applicable)
- Track effectiveness of campaigns
- Can be disabled

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies:
- Analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
- Payment processors
- Security services
- Content delivery networks

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:
- **Browser Settings**: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
- **Our Cookie Preferences**: Use our cookie consent banner
- **Opt-Out Tools**: Industry opt-out tools for advertising cookies

**Note**: Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

## 6. Cookie Retention

- **Session Cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain until expiration or deletion
- **Retention Period**: Varies by cookie type (typically 30 days to 2 years)

We may update this Cookie Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
