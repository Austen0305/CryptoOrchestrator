# Getting Started with CryptoOrchestrator

Welcome to CryptoOrchestrator! This guide will help you get started with the platform in just a few minutes.

## What is CryptoOrchestrator?

CryptoOrchestrator is an AI-powered cryptocurrency trading platform that enables you to:
- **Automate Trading**: Create and deploy trading bots with advanced strategies
- **Copy Trading**: Follow successful traders and copy their trades automatically
- **Portfolio Management**: Track and manage your crypto portfolio across multiple exchanges
- **Advanced Analytics**: Get insights into your trading performance with ML-powered analytics
- **DEX Trading**: Trade directly on decentralized exchanges (DEXs)
- **Risk Management**: Set up automated risk limits and alerts

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Navigate to the signup page
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

### Step 2: Connect an Exchange

1. Go to **Settings** → **Exchange Connections**
2. Click **Add Exchange**
3. Select your exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.)
4. Enter your API credentials (read-only recommended for safety)
5. Test the connection

**Security Tip**: Always use API keys with minimal permissions. For trading bots, use keys with trading permissions but disable withdrawal permissions.

### Step 3: Create Your First Trading Bot

1. Navigate to **Bots** → **Create Bot**
2. Choose a strategy:
   - **Grid Trading**: Buy low, sell high in a price range
   - **DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)**: Buy at regular intervals
   - **Signal Following**: Follow trading signals from providers
3. Configure your bot:
   - Select trading pair (e.g., BTC/USDT)
   - Set investment amount
   - Configure risk parameters
4. Review and activate your bot

### Step 4: Monitor Your Bot

1. Go to **Dashboard** to see your bot's performance
2. View real-time trades and P&L
3. Adjust settings as needed
4. Set up alerts for important events
