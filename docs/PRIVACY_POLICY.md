# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

CryptoOrchestrator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our cryptocurrency trading platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- **Account Information**: Email address, username, password (hashed), first name, last name
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store credit card details
- **Exchange API Keys**: Encrypted and stored securely for trading operations
- **Usage Data**: Trading activity, bot configurations, strategies, portfolio data

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Cookies**: Used for authentication and session management

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Execute trades, manage bots, process subscriptions
2. **Account Management**: Create and maintain user accounts
3. **Payment Processing**: Process subscription payments via Stripe
4. **Security**: Authenticate users, detect fraud, protect against abuse
5. **Communication**: Send account updates, billing notifications, security alerts
6. **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform

## Information Sharing

We do **NOT** sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following cases:

1. **Service Providers**: With trusted third parties (Stripe, hosting providers) who assist in operations
2. **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
3. **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
4. **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data (API keys, passwords) is encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Objection**: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

- Your account is active
- Required to provide our services
- Required by law or to resolve disputes
- You request deletion

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Authentication**: Maintain login sessions
- **Preferences**: Remember user settings
- **Analytics**: Understand platform usage (optional)

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

## Third-Party Services

- **Exchange APIs**: Your chosen cryptocurrency exchanges (see their privacy policies)
